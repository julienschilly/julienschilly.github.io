Lessons

One-on-one sessions tailored to your goals and skill level. We'll make sure you're paired with the best instructor for your personality and learning style.

Ensembles

Join an ensemble to learn, perform, and have fun with other Virtuoso students! Sign up for a random group, or with friends. We offer wind, brass, orchestra, and jazz ensembles each semester; student-created ensembles run for a term.

Theory Courses

Want to support your music education with more comprehensive theory? Take one of our weekly classes and enjoy a hands-on learning environment, small class size, and encouraging instruction that leaves no student behind. Enroll in lessons at Virtuoso and receive a discount on theory courses!

About Virtuoso

Virtuoso Music School was founded with the goal of creating a tight-knit, open-armed community of musicians. We take pride in our local music scene and have had the immense joy of watching it flourish over the years. It is an honor to experience the creative impact that our students make on the world, whether professionally or as a hobby, and our job is to give them the tools to do so.

We look forward to welcoming you into our community, and we can't wait to see where your music education takes you.
